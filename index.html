Joining two pdfs with slightly different page size with pdfjoin under Linux or Mac

    does the trick for me. rotateoversize is the important option
pdfjam --paper a4paper file1.pdf file2.pdf
    did the trick for me using one letter-sized document and one slightly-not-A4-document,
    they're both joined as A4 as expected.
